Hello :)
This application is intended to be used on medium to large screens. It is not developed for phones or smaller screens. The experiment is designed to gain insight on how we respond to a tool most of us encounter every day - the humble webform.
Complete the form by typing your name and email in the appropriate fields. Complete the experiment by submitting the form. you will then be emailed a very brief survey to fill that takes 2-3 minutes.
